About the Project

One System to
Run the Restaurant

Menuo is a comprehensive, AI-powered restaurant management platform that consolidates operations, finances, and analytics into one integrated system. Orders, inventory, staffing, and reporting all live in a single dashboard.

Its AI predicts inventory needs to reduce food waste by up to 40%, while a natural-language assistant lets owners ask questions about their business and get instant answers. Plans range from $299 to $599 per month depending on restaurant size.

The problem it solves: restaurants run on thin margins and a patchwork of disconnected tools. Menuo unifies the back office, surfaces real-time insight, and uses AI to cut waste and speed up decisions — directly protecting the bottom line.
