About the Project

The Field Team’s
Mobile Workspace

The Drevito mobile app is the field-facing companion to the Drevito platform. It puts the full job workflow in the hands of technicians working out on-site, on iOS and Android.

Crews can view their schedule, navigate to the next job, capture photos and notes, and collect payment — all from their phone. The app works offline and syncs in real time with the Drevito web platform, so the office and the field always share the same picture.

The problem it solves: field technicians often work disconnected from the office, leading to lost paperwork, delayed invoicing, and confusion over what’s next. The app keeps them productive anywhere — even with no signal — and closes the loop the moment they’re back online.
