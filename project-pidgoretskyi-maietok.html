Pidgoretskyi Maietok

An 18th-century palace estate, reborn as a luxury hotel, restaurant, and event retreat.

Visit Live Site
About the Project

History Meets
Modern Hospitality

Pidgoretskyi Maietok is a palace-park estate hotel in the village of Pidhirtsi, Lviv region, Ukraine, set within a restored 18th–19th-century complex. The property pairs historic architecture with a modern boutique hotel experience.

Guests enjoy 15 rooms, a restaurant and bar, a full spa zone with a Finnish sauna, Roman steam bath and hydromassage pool, a heated outdoor pool, and landscaped gardens. It's also a sought-after venue for weddings and private events, and holds a 9.4/10 guest rating across hundreds of reviews.

The problem it solves: a destination this distinctive needs a digital presence that conveys its atmosphere and makes booking effortless. The website showcases the estate's rooms, amenities, and event spaces and turns browsing into reservations.
