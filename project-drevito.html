About the Project

The Operating System for
Service Teams

Drevito is a field service management platform that helps service businesses run their day-to-day operations from a single dashboard. Quotes, jobs, customers, scheduling, and billing all live in one connected system.

Instead of juggling spreadsheets, paper job sheets, and separate invoicing tools, teams manage the entire job lifecycle — from the first customer call to the final paid invoice — in Drevito. The office and the field stay perfectly in sync.

The problem it solves: service businesses lose time and revenue to scattered tooling, double data entry, and missed follow-ups. Drevito consolidates the workflow so dispatchers schedule faster, technicians always know their next job, and nothing slips through the cracks.

Key Features

What Drevito
Brings Together

Scheduling & Dispatch

Plan jobs on a visual calendar and dispatch the right technician to the right job in seconds.

Customer & Job History

A built-in CRM keeps every customer, address, and past job a single click away.

Quotes & Invoices

Turn an approved quote into a job and a job into a paid invoice without re-entering a thing.

Payments

Collect payment and reconcile revenue inside the same platform that runs the job.

Real-Time Job Tracking

See job status and team location live, so dispatchers and customers always know what's happening.
