About Us

A Holding Company
Built for Innovation

Fold IT Group is a technology holding company that brings together five specialized digital companies under one roof. We believe in the power of focused expertise combined with unified collaboration.

Each of our companies operates independently with deep specialization in their domain, while sharing resources, knowledge, and a commitment to excellence. This structure allows us to deliver comprehensive digital solutions while maintaining the agility and expertise of specialized teams.

From web and software development to design, secure data infrastructure, and AI — we cover the full spectrum of digital needs with precision and creativity.
